Privacy Policy

At Auronix, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data Attendis collects, why we collect it, and how it's protected.

App: Attendis  ·  Developer: Auronix  ·  Effective Date:  ·  Last Updated:
📋

Overview

Attendis is a self-attendance tracking application developed by Auronix. The app allows users to record and manage their own attendance logs, which are time-stamped and stored for personal reference. This Privacy Policy applies to the Attendis mobile application available on the Google Play Store.

By using Attendis, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

📦

Information We Collect

Attendis does not collect or transmit your personal data to any server. All data you generate stays on your device. The only information involved is:

  • Attendance Records: Check-in and check-out times you log — stored locally on your device only, never uploaded anywhere.
  • Exported Excel Files: Monthly attendance sheets saved to your device's Downloads folder on your request — never uploaded or shared by the app.
  • Ad Identifiers: Anonymous device-level identifiers used by Google AdMob for serving ads (see Advertising section below).
  • App Version Data: The app reads version info and a Play Store URL from Firebase — no user data is involved in this exchange.
We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, or any personally identifiable information. No user-generated data ever leaves your device.
🔥

Firebase Usage

Attendis uses Firebase Realtime Database in a very limited, read-only capacity. It is not used to store any user data. Firebase is used solely for:

  • App version checking: The app reads the latest version number from Firebase to notify you when an update is available.
  • Play Store URL: The app fetches the Play Store link from Firebase to direct you to the update page when needed.
No attendance records, personal information, or any user-generated data is ever sent to or stored in Firebase. All your data lives exclusively on your device.

Firebase may collect basic diagnostic information per Google's own policies. See Firebase Privacy Policy →

📁

Local File Storage

When you export your attendance records, Attendis saves an Excel file directly to your device's Downloads folder. This file:

  • Is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Can be deleted at any time from your device's file manager.
  • Requires the WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE or READ_MEDIA_DOCUMENTS permission depending on your Android version.
  • Is accessible by other apps that have storage permissions on your device.
📢

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Attendis displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to show you relevant ads, which may include:

  • Advertising ID (GAID) — a resettable, anonymous device identifier.
  • Approximate location data (if permitted by your device settings).
  • Interaction data with ads (impressions, clicks).
  • Device information (model, OS version, language).

AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's privacy policy. You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting your device's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For more details, see Google Privacy Policy → and AdMob Data Usage →

🔐

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data handled by Attendis:

  • All your attendance data is stored locally on your device — it never touches our servers.
  • Firebase is only used for read-only version checks, communicated over HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • We do not store any user credentials, passwords, or sensitive information on any server.
  • Locally stored data and exported Excel files are protected by your device's own storage permissions.
Since we don't store your personal data on any server, the risk of a data breach affecting your attendance records is effectively zero.
🤝

Third-Party Services

Attendis integrates with the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase — Used only to check for app updates and fetch the Play Store URL. No user data is stored.
  • Google AdMob — In-app advertising.
  • Google Play Services — App distribution and system-level services.

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any other third parties.

🔔

Local Notifications

Attendis uses local notifications to remind you to record your attendance at scheduled times. These notifications are:

  • Triggered entirely on-device based on times you configure — no data is sent to any server to schedule them.
  • Not linked to any personal identity or account.
  • Only active when you grant the app notification permission on your device.
  • Manageable at any time via your device's Settings → Apps → Attendis → Notifications.
No notification content or scheduling data ever leaves your device. Everything is handled locally by the Android notification system.

  • Delete locally saved Excel files directly from your device.
